About Me

I am a Gameplay Programmer and Computer Engineering graduate from Obafemi Awolowo University. Currently, I work as a Gameplay Programmer at Dimension11 Games, where I develop core combat loops, enemy AI behaviors, and traversal systems for the Action-RPG Legend of Orisha: Blood and Water using Unreal Engine 5 and C++.

My work bridges the gap between gameplay engineering and game design. I focus on writing highly optimized C++ code, designing extensible Blueprint systems, and implementing backend services using tools like Azure PlayFab. Over the years, I've also led game development initiatives as a Google Developer Student Clubs Lead and Microsoft Student Learn Ambassador.

I am available for freelance projects, collaborations, and full-time opportunities in Video Game and Media.
